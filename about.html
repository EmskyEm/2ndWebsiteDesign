Ema is a self-taught Slovak photographer that specialises in portrait, editorial, and children photography. She has created and collected an international body of work throughout the years of shooting, directing creative teams, and working on independent projects with professional artists and models.
She is currently based between United Kingdom and Barcelona.

Years of creative experimentation and customer satisfaction resulted in authentic images presented in three photographic categories. Ema believes that quality photographs require a particular organisation and communication, where understanding the diversity of people and life around us is essentially what completes a successful photo-shoot. Emotions and people change, a precious moment in a photograph does not.
